About our project

Welcome to Tampere Developers' Portal! The site is developed and operated by the City's Open Data team.

You can find information about our projects, open data sets and APIs. All data is open and freely distributed as CC4.0. All code samples and APIs are available in GitHub and licensed as CC4.0.
